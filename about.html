Goals before leaving MIT

When I was younger, I had an MIT hat hang on my nightstand next to my bed to remind me of my ultimate goal, which was to get into MIT. Now that that has been accomplished, I have a bunch of goals that I would like to accomplish by the time I end MIT. I’d like to start my own business before I leave, I’d like to at the least get my Master’s Degree here, and I’d like to TA a class just to name a few. I’d also like to be a coordinator for Camp Kesem and win a NEWMAC Championship with soccer - but most of all, I’d just like to look back on my years at MIT with a ton of amazing memories, as these years I know will be some of the best of my life.
