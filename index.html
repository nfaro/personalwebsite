Camp Kesem

"Kesem is a nationwide community, driven by passionate college student leaders, that supports children through and beyond their parent's cancer. By offering innovative, fun‐filled programs that foster a lasting community, we aim to ensure that every child impacted by a parent's cancer is never alone." - Kesem Nationals
As a counselor at CK, I work year‐round to prepare for a magical week of camp that creates memories that both campers and counselors alike will never forget. It is free for the campers from our fundraising, and gives them a time to step away from struggles at home and just be a kid. It is by far the most fufilling thing I've ever done!

Soccer

Ever since I can remember, I've had a ball at my feet. Now I play for the MIT Men's Varisty Team, and I love every second of it. I couldn't imagine my life without soccer, and I'm grateful to have the opportunity to play at such a high level.

Phi Beta Epsilon

When I came to MIT, I immediately found my friends for life and the best support system I could ask for in Phi Beta Epsilon. With an incredibly extensive alumni network, a beautiful house, and high expectations for success, it has provided me with the perfect environment to thrive.
